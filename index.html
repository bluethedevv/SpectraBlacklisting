About SpectraBlacklisting

SpectraBlacklisting is a community-driven protection system designed to keep Discord and Roblox communities safe from scammers and malicious actors.

Our system uses real-time blacklisting to prevent known scammers from causing harm across multiple communities. The database starts empty and grows only with verified reports.

No pre-filled data. We believe in starting fresh and only adding confirmed malicious actors based on actual incidents.
