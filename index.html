About SpectraBlacklisting

SpectraBlacklisting is a community-driven protection system designed to keep Discord and Roblox communities safe from scammers and malicious actors.

Our system uses real-time blacklisting to prevent known scammers from causing harm across multiple communities. The database is maintained by trusted administrators and is accessible to all partnered servers.

No fake data. We only track and list confirmed malicious actors based on verified reports from trusted sources.
